FAQ

Frequenty asked questions

What types of issues can I report?

You can report a variety of issues including waste collection problems, drainage blockages, road damage (such as potholes), and non-functioning or damaged street lights. Each section of the website is designed to handle specific types of reports.

Who receives my report once it’s submitted?

Your report is automatically directed to the relevant municipal department or authority responsible for handling the specific issue you’ve reported. This ensures that your concern is addressed by the appropriate team.

How long does it take to resolve an issue?

The resolution time can vary depending on the type of issue and its urgency. Our platform ensures that all reports are prioritized and addressed as quickly as possible. You can monitor the status of your report for updates.

Is the website available in multiple languages?

Currently, the website is available in [list languages if applicable]. We are working on adding more language options to accommodate a wider range of users.

Is my personal information safe when I submit a report?

Yes, your personal information is protected with the highest level of security. We use encryption and secure protocols to ensure your data is kept confidential and safe from unauthorized access.

What should I do if my waste hasn't been collected on the scheduled day?

If your waste has not been collected as scheduled, report the issue through our Waste Management section on the website. Provide details such as your address and any photos of the uncollected waste, and we will ensure it is addressed promptly.
